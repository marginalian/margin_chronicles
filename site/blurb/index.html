Margin Chronicles

Welcome


This is a digital commonplace book full of personal reading notes. It is divided into chapters on different subjects. Take, for example, the chapter on science fiction writing. It contains a chapter index that contextualizes several pages of extended marginalia. That marginalia, in turn, relies on a set of reading notes.

The fledgling chapter about sailing follows the same pattern.

If your interests overlap mine, then you might find something of value here. But, this project does not always aim to provide clean, public-facing content, so some of the chapters will be incomplete and many of the pages are rough around the edges.

At its core, this project is just a collection of domain-specific markup languages and embedded lisp. A custom static-site generator transforms those files into the site you're reading now. If you are interested to learn more about the mechanics of how this site works, the MMX chapter is a good place to start.

At last count, this site contained 59 pages and 406 reading notes.

Margin Chronicles participates in a webring of non-commercial websites.
