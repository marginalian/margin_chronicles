Summary Sheet

Plot Evaluation

  • What is the central dramatic question? I.e. What question keeps the reader reading? Usually connected to the implicit question in almost every story: what happens to the protagonist?
  • What is the throughline? The throughline is the answer to the dramatic question. How is it structured?

Scene Evaluation

There are 2 possible functions of a scene, according to Nancy Kress: develop character or advance plot.

Advancing the plot:

  • introducing a force that will support the climax
  • examining any element that generated conflict
