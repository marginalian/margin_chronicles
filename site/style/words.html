Writing Terms

Episodic Plot

In an episodic plot, events are knit together less by causality than by thier common relationship to some central event or character or theme.
 Paragons - Robin Wilson

Contrast with organic plots.

Organic Plot

An organic plot is based upon direct and perceptible relationships between events, each being linked to its predecessor and successor by strong bonds of causality.
 Paragons - Robin Wilson

Contrast with episodic plots.

Spear Carrier

A background character. A walk-on part (on screen).
