Words

Interwingled

[adj] (of knowledge) to be interconnected or interrelated in a fundamental way.

Everything is deeply interwingled. The only way in which my views differ with those of Engelbart and Pask, I think is in the matter of structure and hierarchy. Both men generally assume that whatever natural hierarchy may exist in particular subjects needs to be accentuated; I hold that all structures must be treated as totally arbitrary, and any hierarchies we find are interesting accidents.
 Computer Lib/Dream Machines - Ted Nelson

Stentorian

[adj] (of a person's voice) loud and powerful.

Etymology: Stentor was the herald of the Greek forces in the Iliad, noted for his loud voice.
